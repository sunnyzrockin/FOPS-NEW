# FOPS — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2 July 2026
**Last updated:** 2 July 2026

FOPS ("**we**", "**us**", "**our**") is operated by **New Elite Ventures Pty Ltd** (ABN **96 678 447 384**). We're committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in line with the **Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)** and the **Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)**.

This policy explains what we collect, why, how we handle it, and your rights. It covers both our website/waitlist (**fopsapp.com**) and the FOPS application.

---

## 1. What we collect

**If you join our waitlist:**
- Your email address (required)
- Optionally, your name and business details (e.g. number of sites)

**If you use the FOPS application (e.g. as a beta tester):**
- Account information — name, email, and your role (owner, operator, or staff)
- Business operational data you enter — site details, shift reports, banking and sales figures, fuel stock and tank readings, fuel prices
- Technical data — log and usage information, and error diagnostics, collected automatically to keep the service running

**Payments:** if and when billing is enabled, card payments are processed by **Stripe**. We do **not** store your full card number — Stripe handles that directly.

We don't collect sensitive information (as defined in the Privacy Act) and ask that you don't enter it into the app.

---

## 2. How we collect it
- **Directly from you** — when you join the waitlist, create or accept an account, or enter data into the app.
- **Automatically** — service logs and error-monitoring data generated as you use the app.

---

## 3. Why we collect it and how we use it
We use your information to:
- Contact you about FOPS early access (waitlist)
- Provide, operate, and secure the FOPS service
- Process billing (when enabled)
- Provide support and respond to your requests
- Diagnose problems and improve the product

We only use your information for these purposes, or as otherwise permitted by the Privacy Act. We don't sell your information, and we don't use it for unrelated advertising.

---

## 4. Who we share it with
We share information only with service providers that help us run FOPS, and only as needed:
- **Supabase** — database and authentication
- **Vercel** — application hosting
- **Stripe** — payment processing (when billing is enabled)
- **Sentry** — error monitoring

These providers are bound to handle your information securely and only for the services they provide to us. We don't otherwise disclose your information unless required by law.

---

## 5. Overseas disclosure
Some of our service providers (including those above) store or process data on servers **outside Australia** (for example, in the United States). By using FOPS, you consent to this overseas handling. We take reasonable steps to ensure these providers protect your information consistently with the APPs.

---

## 6. How we protect your information
We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and database-level row security that scopes each user's access to only their own data. No system is perfectly secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security — but we work to reduce risk and respond quickly to any issue.

---

## 7. Data breaches
If a data breach occurs that is likely to result in serious harm, we will assess it and notify affected individuals and the **Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)** as required under the **Notifiable Data Breaches** scheme.

---

## 8. Accessing and correcting your information
You can ask us to access or correct the personal information we hold about you, or to delete it (for example, to be removed from the waitlist), at any time. Contact us at **privacy@arcera.com.au** and we'll respond within a reasonable time. We may need to verify your identity first.

---

## 9. How long we keep it
We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law. Waitlist details are kept until you ask to be removed or we close the waitlist. Account and business data is kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period after, then deleted or de-identified.

---

## 10. Cookies and analytics
We use only the essential cookies needed to operate the app and keep you signed in. We don't currently use analytics or advertising cookies. If that changes, we'll update this policy.

---

## 11. Complaints
If you have a privacy concern, contact us first at **privacy@arcera.com.au** and we'll work to resolve it. If you're not satisfied, you can contact the **Office of the Australian Information Commissioner** at **oaic.gov.au**.

---

## 12. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We'll post the updated version here with a new "last updated" date, and notify you of material changes where appropriate.
